Solviz: Example

This demonstration visualizes how the ASP solver clingo carries out its search on logic programs.

Select an example logic program, or enter your own.
Select the demonstration speed, and click "Run!" to start.

When the demonstration runs, various events are shown, such as branching decisions, propagation of literals, and reversal of previously set literals in the backtracking and backjumping process.
